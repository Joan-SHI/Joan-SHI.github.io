My reflections on time and habit techniques

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I did try Pomodoro techniques and it seems work for me. Every 25mins I have a break(5mins). Then after 4 rounds(4*25mins)I have a longer break(20mins). Time seems fly quicker than when I haven't tried Pomodoro techniques. For growth mindset, when I recalled my net volley of tennis, I did gain much confidence through actions(picked up more balls and returned some good shots).

What did you find interesting?

This saying"Confidence is just displayed ability" really hit me. And my previous experience tells me that it is true. If we displayed our ability,then the Confidence would come. I am going to use my actions to drive my mindset from now on.

What worked for you?

Pomodoro techniques and Growth mindset worked for me.
