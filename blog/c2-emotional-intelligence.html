How did you find the 2-mins of mindfulness?

I seem lose the ability of "just be" which I had when I was a kid. It seems easy to get lost when busy "doing things",doesn't it? Let's shift from "doing" to "being" for just 2 mins then come back to the real world. It is a good way for me to refresh myself.
