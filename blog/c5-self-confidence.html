What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are essentially same thing according to the following definitions:

"Self-awareness is a neutral mode that maintains self-reflectiveness even in the midst of turbulent emotions."By Daniel Goleman

“Mindfulness means paying attention in a particular way: on purpose, in the present moment, and non-judgmentally.”By Jon Kabat-Zinn
