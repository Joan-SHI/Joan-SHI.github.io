How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

One interesting feature about these three lens is their varying sustainability.

  • Life through pleasure len:This type of happiness is about always chasing the next high which means it is highly unsustainable.
  • Life through passion len:Happiness that arises from passion is much more sustainable and you are far less likely to habituate to it.
  • Life through higher purpose len:This is about being part of something bigger than yourself that has meaning to you. Thus, this form of happiness is very resilient and highly sustainable.
